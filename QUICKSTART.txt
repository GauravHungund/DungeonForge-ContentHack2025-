🎉 AI Dungeon Master is RUNNING!

✅ Backend: http://localhost:8001 (Flask API)
✅ Frontend: http://localhost:3000 (React App)

📝 How it works:
1. Type a message in the chat
2. Message goes to your Airia agent
3. Get an epic dungeon master story back!

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Add your Airia credentials to .env file:
   AIRIA_API_KEY=your-airia-api-key-here
   AIRIA_USER_ID=your-user-id-here

🚀 Open your browser to: http://localhost:3000

Try typing something like:
"You wake up in a mysterious cave filled with glowing crystals..."

📚 For detailed setup: See AIRIA_SETUP.md
